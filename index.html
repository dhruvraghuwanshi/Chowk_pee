About ChowkPe

What is ChowkPe?

We envision a world where migrant workers are recognized for their skills and contributions, where employers have seamless access to a verified and ready workforce, and where both parties can engage in a transparent, efficient, and mutually beneficial manner.

Introducing ChowkPe

Workforce

Find Opportunity

Experience a seamless job search and career growth with flexible location and work category with the ChowkPe Workers App. Our easy registration process allows you to quickly sign up and create a verified profile that highlights your skills. Explore and apply for jobs that match your abilities and preferences, ensuring you find the best opportunities available.

Improve Productivity with features

Our Voice Assist feature guides users through the registration process, ensuring a seamless experience from start to finish. The dashboard enhances transparency by allowing workers to check ESI and PF details, view their work calendar, access payment slips, track applied jobs, receive technical support, and monitor their progress. Our app also helps workers build a resume-like profile, improving their credibility and establishing a professional identity. Additionally, our gamified digital learning feature boosts productivity by visualizing worksite processes, facilitating better understanding and engagement.

HR Recruiters

Hire Workers

Simplify your recruitment process with the ChowkPe Employers App. Easily register and manage your employer profile, and effortlessly post and manage job listings. Our intuitive three-step shortlisting process helps you filter and select the best candidates based on their verified profiles and skills in your line of work, in your industry, and your locations. Experience easier on-boarding, improved cash flow, and less paperwork.

Improve Management

Seamlessly integrate with your existing HR management system for a streamlined experience. Simplify payroll and attendance tracking with our integrated tools, ensuring accuracy and efficiency. Access dedicated support whenever you need assistance. Join ChowkPe today and connect with a skilled and reliable workforce to drive your business forward.
